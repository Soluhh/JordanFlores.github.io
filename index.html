Projects

Tilevania

Tilevania is a short 2D platformer where you collect coins, crush your enemies, and shoot clone projectiles at your enemies.

Galooga

Galooga is a 2D Galaga inspired clone where you battle infinite waves of enemies and try to achieve the highest score possible.

Pongu

Pongu is a 2D Pong inspired clone where you can choose to face an AI or choose to play against a friend to see who can score five points first.

Naru-Trivia

Naru-Trivia is a 2D trivia game based on the topic of Naruto where you get questioned on your knowledge of the Naruto universe.

FrootSlash

FrootSlash is a 3D Fruit Ninja inspired clone where you test your skills to see how high of a score you can get while avoiding bombs.

TDThrones

TDThrones is 3D survival tower defense game where you have to strategize and plan out the best positions for your towers to defend your castle.

SparrowDefender

SparrowDefender is a 3D hardcore railsystem game where you have to defend your homeland against the invading enemies trying to destroy your homeland.

SnowySlopes

SnowySlopes is 2D snow boarding game where you have to reach the finish line with or without style while also trying not to hit your head on the immense hills you traverse.

MailMan

MailMan is a short 2D game where your job is to deliver the blue packages one at a time to the yellow square while trying not to lose speed crashing into obstacles.

RocketBoost

RocketBoost is a 3D game where you have to reach your destination without crashing into any obstacles on your way to your goal.
